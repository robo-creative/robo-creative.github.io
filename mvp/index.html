Separation of Concerns

Simplify development and maintenance of your applications. Make your components truely resuable. Make independent development better than ever.

Tackle complexity in the heart of your applications with Passive View and Supervising Controller. Choose your favourite variant with freedom, or go with both of them.

Make your own extensions in just few steps and share it with community. Easily replace any part with yours to satisfy complex business requirements.

Exploring Demo Center

The Demo Center provides overview information of every feature in Robo MVP and sample implementation. All demos ship with brief description, full source code for both Java and Layouts, useful in learning how to use framework functionality.
